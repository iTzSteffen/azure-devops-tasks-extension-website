Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 19, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Azure DevOps Tasks Chrome Extension ("I", "my", or "the extension") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use my browser extension. I am committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws.

Data Controller

I, Steffen Hofbauer, am the data controller for the personal data collected through this extension. As the data controller, I determine the purposes and means of processing your personal data.

Information I Collect

The Azure DevOps Tasks Chrome Extension collects the following information:

  • Azure DevOps Personal Access Tokens (PATs) - provided by you for authentication with Azure DevOps services
  • Azure DevOps Organization and Project names - to connect to your Azure DevOps instances
  • Your Azure DevOps username - to identify pull requests where you are mentioned
  • User preferences - including display settings, theme preferences, and custom shortcuts

Legal Grounds for Processing

I process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Contractual Necessity: Processing is necessary for the performance of the service you requested.
  • Legitimate Interest: Processing is necessary for my legitimate interests in providing and improving the extension.
  • Consent: Where required, I will obtain your explicit consent before processing your data.

How I Use Your Information

I use the information I collect solely for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate with Azure DevOps services on your behalf
  • To retrieve and display your task and pull request data
  • To provide personalized features (such as highlighting pull requests where you are mentioned)
  • To store your preferences for the extension

Data Retention

I retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the service and fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy. Specifically:

  • Your Personal Access Tokens are stored until you revoke them or uninstall the extension
  • Your preferences are stored until you change them or uninstall the extension
  • All data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the extension

Data Storage

All data collected by the extension is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's secure storage mechanisms:

  • Your Personal Access Tokens and connection information are stored in Chrome's sync storage
  • Your preferences and settings are stored in Chrome's sync storage
  • No data is stored on my servers

Data Sharing

I do not share your data with any third parties. The extension communicates directly between your browser and Azure DevOps services. No information passes through my servers.

Social Media

I maintain a presence on the following social media platforms:

  • GitHub: For code hosting, issue tracking, and community interaction

When you interact with me on these platforms, your interactions are subject to the respective platform's privacy policies. I do not collect or store any personal data from these platforms beyond what is necessary to respond to your inquiries or contributions.

Security

I implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:

  • All communication with Azure DevOps is performed over HTTPS
  • Your Personal Access Token is stored securely in Chrome's storage and is only used to authenticate with Azure DevOps
  • I do not have access to any of the data stored by the extension

Your Rights Under GDPR

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact me using the information provided below.
